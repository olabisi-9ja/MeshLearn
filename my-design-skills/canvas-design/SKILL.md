# Canvas Design Skill

Focuses strictly on architectural aesthetic balance, geometric forms, colors, and spatial composition.

## Core Directives

* Prioritize structural whitespace and strong visual hierarchies over dense text blocks.
* Formulate static design pieces as carefully crafted art layouts following Swiss or Modern layout design rules.
* Leverage structural layouts to direct ocular focus across core elements.
